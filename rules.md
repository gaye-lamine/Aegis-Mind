# Splunk Agentic Ops Hackathon Official Rules

## 1. Dates and Timing
*   **Registration Period:** Mar 27, 2026 12:00 PM PDT – Jun 15, 2026 9:00 AM PDT
*   **Submission Period:** May 18, 2026 9:00 AM PDT – Jun 15, 2026 9:00 AM PDT
*   **Feedback Period:** May 18, 2026 9:00 AM PDT – Jun 19, 2026 9:00 AM PDT
*   **Judging Period:** Jun 26, 2026 9:00 AM PDT – Jul 10, 2026 5:00 PM PDT
*   **Winners Announced:** On or around Jul 17, 2026 2:00 PM PDT

## 2. Sponsor and Administrator
*   **Sponsor:** Cisco Systems, Inc, 170 West Tasman Drive, San Jose, CA 95134
*   **Administrator:** Devpost, Inc., 250 Broadway, Floor 24, New York, NY 10007

## 3. Eligibility
*   **Eligible Individuals:** At least the age of majority where they reside.
*   **Teams:** Up to 2 individuals.
*   **Organizations:** Legal entities organized or incorporated at the time of entry.
*   **Exclusions:** Residents of Belarus, Brazil, Donetsk People’s Republic, Luhansk People’s Republic, Quebec, Russia, Crimea, Cuba, Iran, Syria, North Korea, and other OFAC-sanctioned countries. Government or state-owned entities. Sponsor and Devpost employees.

## 4. How To Enter
1.  **Register:** Sign up at [splunk.devpost.com](https://splunk.devpost.com).
2.  **Access Tools:** Create a free Splunk account, download the Splunk Enterprise Trial (60 days), and apply for a Developer License (6 months) at [dev.splunk.com](https://dev.splunk.com/).
3.  **Provide Feedback:** Submit the online feedback form during the Feedback Period to qualify for the Most Valuable Feedback prizes.
4.  **Submit Project:** Fill out all required fields on the "Enter a Submission" page on Devpost during the Submission Period.

### Project Requirements
*   **What to Create:** Build an innovative AI-powered solution leveraging one or more of Splunk’s latest AI capabilities to solve real-world challenges in observability, security operations, or developer productivity.
*   **Tracks:**
    *   **Observability:** AI-powered solutions for system behavior, anomaly detection, operational responses.
    *   **Security:** Threat detection, efficient investigation, security workflow automation using AI and Splunk.
    *   **Platform & Developer Experience:** Simplify interaction with Splunk data/APIs, automate workflows.
*   **Functionality:** Must run consistently and perform as depicted in the video/text.
*   **New & Existing:** Projects must be newly created or significantly updated after the start of the Submission Period.
*   **Third-Party Integrations:** Authorized use is mandatory.

### Submission Requirements
*   **Track Identification:** Must declare track (Observability, Security, or Platform & DevEx).
*   **Text Description:** Explaining features and functionality.
*   **Demo Video:** Less than three (3) minutes, uploaded to YouTube/Vimeo/Youku, showing the project functioning, showing AI usage, and explaining the value/problem. No unlicensed material.
*   **Public Repository URL:** Detectable open-source license file, clear README, setup and run instructions, required dependencies, example configurations.
*   **Architecture Diagram:** A file named `architecture_diagram.(md|pdf|png)` at the root of the repository showing:
    *   Interaction with Splunk.
    *   How AI models/agents are integrated.
    *   Data flow between components.

## 5. Submission Modifications
*   Drafts can be modified until the Submission Period ends.
*   No changes are allowed after the deadline unless specifically permitted by Sponsor/Devpost for safety, IP infringement, or privacy fixes.

## 6. Judges & Criteria
*   **Stage One:** Pass/fail screening for baseline viability and alignment with the hackathon theme.
*   **Stage Two (Equally Weighted):**
    *   **Technological Implementation:** Quality software development.
    *   **Design:** User experience and thoughtfully designed interface.
    *   **Potential Impact:** Size of potential impact.
    *   **Quality of the Idea:** Creativity and uniqueness.

## 7. Intellectual Property Rights
*   Entrants retain full ownership of their intellectual property.
*   Sponsor is granted a non-exclusive license to use the entry for judging and publicity purposes (up to three years).
